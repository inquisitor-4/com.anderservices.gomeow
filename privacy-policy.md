# Privacy Policy for GoMeow App

**Last Updated:** November 2025

## 1. Information We Collect

Our game "GoMeow" is designed with complete anonymity in mind. We do not collect any personal data from our users.

## 2. Game Data

The game stores your gameplay progress on our servers to enable synchronization between devices. This includes:

- Game level and results
- Game achievements
- Game statistics

All data is anonymized and does not contain personal information. We cannot identify specific users by the stored data.

## 3. Advertising

We display advertisements in our game through Google's AdMob service. AdMob may collect anonymous data for:

- Showing relevant advertisements
- Evaluating ad effectiveness
- Analytics and optimization of advertising campaigns

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- AdMob by Google - for displaying advertisements
- Our own servers - for storing gameplay progress

## 5. Data Security

All game data is stored in anonymized form. We use standard security measures to protect information on our servers.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age.

## 7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by publishing the new Privacy Policy on this page.
